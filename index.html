M4Fog: A Global Multi-Regional, Multi-Modal, and Multi-Stage Dataset for Marine Fog Detection and Forecasting to Bridge Ocean and Atmosphere
Marine fog significantly impacts global shipping, necessitating effective detection and forecasting. The M4Fog dataset, comprising nearly a decade of multi-modal data from geostationary satellites, offers comprehensive resources for developing ML-driven solutions to mitigate these impacts.
